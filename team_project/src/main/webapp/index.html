Whether you are in need of a tech resource to take online school or want to contribute to students in need, PROJECT_NAME can help you with that.

About the project

The COVID-19 pandemic has forced most students into online learning, but not everyone has the same opportunity to access the technologies needed to take online classes. We are aware of that, so we created this tool to connect people who want to offer their help and those students in need of the resources.

Quick & easy to use
